Wildcards

To match words with a range of lengths, use an asterisk known as a Wildcard. An asterisk in a pattern represents zero or more unknown letters.

Wildcards can be used in either the template part of a pattern or the anagram part of a pattern: in a template, the unknown letters must appear in sequence where the asterisk appears; in an anagram, the unknown letters can be positioned anywhere in the word and the letters used to replace the unknowns are shown after a + in Anagram Balances.
