Templates

The simplest form of TEA pattern is a Template showing the known letters in a word. You can use a dot (.) or question mark (?) as the Match-All Character that indicates the unknown letters in the template.

Templates are useful for finding answers to complete a crossword, when several letters are already known.

Examples

..... shows all five letter words 
abaci
aback
abaft
... etc ...
q???k shows five letter words starting
with a Q and ending with a K 
quack
quark
quick
quirk
