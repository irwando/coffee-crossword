Other Software You Might Like

If you enjoy using TEA, you might also like Ross Beresford’s other word game software. For further information, click on the links below:

Sympathy Crossword Construction

Sympathy helps you create professional quality crosswords for publication in printed form or on the web. Its features appeal to both compilers and publishers of crossword puzzles.

Crossword Wordplay Wizard

Wordplay Wizard helps you write clues for cryptic crosswords. It shows the ways an answer can be formed from other words by anagramming, reversal and other types of wordplay commonly used in cryptic clues.

BanderSnatch Word Game

Make long words from adjacent tiles in the heap at the bottom of the BanderSnatch game board. While you are doing this, new tiles are being added at an ever-increasing rate. This addictive game should appeal to word puzzle enthusiasts of all ages.
