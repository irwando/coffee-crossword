Overview of Dictionaries

The default Dictionaries are organized into a hierarchy, with the most common vocabulary being searched first and the least common last. The advantage of this is that the most likely results are shown in the top pane and the least likely in the bottom pane.

Click on the links below for further information about each dictionary:

Dictionary Name Entries (approx.) Vocabulary
Core English 67,000 The most common words and phrases in English.
Edited English 265,000 The words and phrases most likely to appear in puzzles.
Unedited English 1,045,000 All the available words and phrases. This dictionary also includes all the available Dictionary Definitions and Citations.
Wikipedia English 4,835,000 Article titles from the English version of Wikipedia

If you are evaluating TEA, there is only one dictionary available called Evaluation. It includes around 65,000 words with Dictionary Definitions.

The following extra dictionaries are available, but not opened by default. Click on the links below for further information about each dictionary:

Dictionary Name Entries (approx.) Vocabulary
Anagrams 45,000 Anagrammable entries from Edited English
Reversibles 2,000 Reversible entries from Edited English
Letter Banks 45,000 Letter banks from Edited English
