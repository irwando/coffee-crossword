The Edited English Dictionary includes a much wider vocabulary than Core English. It consists of the 265,000 (approx.) words and phrases most likely to be used in puzzles (in the opinion of the editor). Please report any errors and omissions to support@crosswordman.com.

Many words and phrases in Edited English have a full Dictionary Definition in Unedited English. Where a Definition wasn't available, the editor has (in most cases) included a Citation to one of the following sources where the entry may be found:
