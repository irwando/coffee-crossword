Core English Dictionary Licensed Version Only

The Core English Dictionary includes approximately 67,000 of the most common words and phrases in English.

Each word or phrase is included in at least two ESL (English as a Second Language) and "desk" dictionaries. This was the approach Alan Beale used to derive a core vocabulary of American English for his "12dicts" project. Core English is based on 2of12.txt from version 3.0 of 12dicts.

Every word or phrase in Core English has a Dictionary Definition in Unedited English.
