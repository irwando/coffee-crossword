Welcome to TEA Crossword Helper

TEA helps you find words that match the pattern you’re looking for. It searches English language word lists with over 5 million unique entries as standard. You can incorporate your own word lists using the TEA&Sympathy Dictionary Builder.

Using the flexibility of TEA patterns, you can devise hundreds of different types of searches: the basic features of patterns are simple to understand; the power of TEA comes from combining these building blocks into more complex patterns.

Some of the tasks TEA can be used for are (click on the links for more information and examples):

  • finding answers to complete a crossword (Templates)
  • solving anagram clues (Anagrams)
  • matching words of unknown length (Wildcards)
  • matching words where a letter is from a restricted set (Choice Lists)
  • finding long words from a set of letters (Templates with Anagrams)
  • finding words with unusual properties such as Palindromes (Letter Variables)
  • finding words punctuated in a particular way (Punctuation Matching)
  • finding words that include specified words in their Dictionary Definition (Searching for Definitions)
  • finding words satisfy multiple criteria (Logical Operations)
  • finding words with unusual patterns of vowels and consonants (Macros)
  • matching words that include another pattern (Sub-Patterns)

For further help about any TEA window or dialog, press the F1 key or click on the Help button.
